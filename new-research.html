    Age has nothing to do with it.

    Better Than Birthdays is the newest and most-ambitious research study to-date from DAVID ALLISON INC. It proves conclusively that how old we are has nothing to do with how we behave.

    Data from 40,000 surveys x 300 questions across the USA and Canada reveals that targeting Millennials or Boomers, or any age-based audience, is unproductive at best, and very likely does more harm than good. Eliminating age-based targeting is one thing, but what's a better system?

    The Better Than Birthdays study reveals ten audience profiles that outperform traditional age based demographic segmentation models. For marketing, advertising, design, culture, planning, product development...this changes everything.

    Do you want to build a bigger and more engaged audience? A new product or brand designed to appeal to a wider target group? An internal corporate culture that can attract better employees and keep them happier?

    Whatever your goals are, this new research will change the way you do business, and it has the potential to change the world.
