    Motivating Your Market

    Workshops

    We help organizations all over the world motivate the audiences they are trying to reach.

    1. We create a Valuegraphics Profile for a specific target audience.

    2. Next, we design and facilitate a workshop to help our clients understand the data, and develop ways to implement the findings together.

    3. We deliver a final Valuegraphics Report, which can be used to brief internal teams and external consultants.

    This process takes approximately 10 weeks, and is customized for each engagement.

    Contact us for more details.

    Custom Research

    We also routinely design, launch and analyze custom surveys to learn more about a market, an audience, a strategy or a brand.

    Our surveys and data analysis can stand alone, or be integrated into a workshop/presentation for your organization.
