Speaker, author, advisor, and researcher David Allison helps us decipher what’s going on around us, and prepare for what’s coming next.

He founded DAVID ALLISON INC to help organizations create products and campaigns for a new world where age is irrelevant, and entire industries need to be reimagined as technology makes current models obsolete.

After more than 30 years working with organizations all over the world, David has earned a few fans. He's been called a “force of nature” by CEO's, and a “born storyteller” by best-selling authors. Famous architects have called him a “@#)%)@ genius”, and senior executives across North America say he's “the best at what he does, period.”

David Allison is passionate about the reinvention of contemporary life, and how human beings are changing as a result.

He discovered common traits shared by boomers and millennials (and everyone in-between), and surveyed over 35,000 people to find the most motivating ways to reach them.

He’s helped plan and design better living in cities, offices, and condominiums.

He eases the anxiety of traditional sectors as they face new technologies. He even wrote a book to help large corporations prepare for the impact of social media before anyone else was paying attention.

He works with groups as wide-ranging as universities, art galleries, and aboriginal nations, to city-builders, tech companies and investment bankers, and carries insights between them.
